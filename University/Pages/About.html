About CKSVIM

CKSVIM at Glance

C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is one of the top management college in the heart of Vadodara city in Gujarat state, founded by 110+ years old Shri Mahavira Jain Vidyalaya (SMJV) trust (Mumbai).

CKSVIM offers Global Master of Business Administration (MBA) two-year full time Post-Graduate Degree programme approved by AICTE, MHRD, Govt. of India and Affiliated to Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Ahmedabad.

Mission

To develop global leaders with ethical and entrepreneurial spirit.

Vision

To be the Institution of preferred choice of the students in the chosen field of Management Education.

DR. KERAV PANDYA

Director, C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management

At C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM), Vadodara, our foremost commitment is to create a safe, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating environment that supports the holistic development of our students and staff.

Our goal is to be an institution of excellence in management education and collaborative research, with a deep commitment to people. We continuously strengthen our academic framework to ensure quality, rigor, and relevance in line with evolving industry and business needs.

The vision of CKSVIM is to impart professional education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and ability required to successfully address the challenges of contemporary and transnational organizations.

From the outset, we have emphasized outcome-based curriculum design, experienced faculty with strong academic and innovative teaching‚Äìlearning practices that enhance engagement and learning effectiveness. We believe that learning should be meaningful, practical, and enjoyable.

Our academic programs are supported by structured placement and career advisory services aimed at shaping students into confident professionals and future business leaders. Through industry engagement and collaborative initiatives, we strive to create value for learners, organizations, and society.

CKSVIM assures parents, students, and society as whole that every effort is made to nurture capable, ethical, and confident professionals who are well prepared to meet the challenges and opportunities of the future.
