C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is a premier management institute located in the heart of Vadodara, Gujarat. Established by the renowned Shri Mahavira Jaina Vidyalaya (SMJV) Trust, Mumbai, with a legacy of over 100 years, CKSVIM is committed to academic excellence and professional development.

The institute offers quality management education through programs approved by AICTE and the Ministry of Education, Government of India, and is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU) and Shri Govind Guru University (SGGU).
