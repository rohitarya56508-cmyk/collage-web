C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is a premier management institute located in the heart of Vadodara, Gujarat. Established by the renowned Shri Mahavira Jaina Vidyalaya (SMJV) Trust, Mumbai, with a legacy of over 100 years, CKSVIM is committed to academic excellence and professional development.

The institute offers quality management education through programs approved by AICTE and the Ministry of Education, Government of India, and is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU) and Shri Govind Guru University (SGGU).

BAOU Study Centre

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Open University

SMJVs C K Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is an Approved Study Centre of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Open University since August 2019. BAOU is recognized by Distance Education Council (DEC).

Post Graduate

MBA

2 years Full-Time MBA Programme

Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate degree program focused on developing managerial, analytical and leadership competencies in the field of business administration.

C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is an AICTE-recognized institute affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Ahmedabad, committed to delivering quality management education aligned with industry requirements.

Post Graduate

BBA

3 years Full Time BBA Programme

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a highly in-demand and market-oriented undergraduate degree that equips students with essential managerial, analytical and entrepreneurial skills for the business world.

C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is an AICTE-recognized institute affiliated with Shri Govind Guru University (SGGU), offering a comprehensive and industry-focused BBA program.

Post Graduate

B. Voc (BFSI)

3 Years Full time Regular Program

Bachelor of Vocation (B.Voc) in Banking, Financial Services & Insurance (BFSI) is a highly industry-oriented undergraduate program designed to build specialized professional skills for the banking and financial sector.

The program focuses on practical knowledge, analytical abilities and job-ready competencies in areas such as banking operations, financial services, insurance, investment planning and risk management.

C. K. Shah Vijapurwala Institute of Management (CKSVIM) is an AICTE-recognized institute affiliated with Gujarat Technological University (GTU), offering a comprehensive and industry-aligned B.Voc BFSI program to prepare students for strong career opportunities in the BFSI domain.
